Join our team

We are always looking for talented, curious people who want to work at the intersection of geoscience, physics, and AI.

Please reach out if:

  • You have analysis-ready datasets on extreme climate or environmental hazards.
  • You have physics-based methods to predict the dynamics or consequences of these hazards that would benefit from computational speed-up using physics-aware AI surrogate models.
  • You have a compelling research idea at this intersection, with interesting literature and data to explore.
