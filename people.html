Join Our Team

We are always looking for talented and enthusiastic individuals to join our team!

Please reach out if:

  • You have analysis-ready datasets about extreme natural climate or environmental hazards
  • You have physics-based methods to predict the dynamics, or consequences, of these hazards that would benefit from computational speed-up using physics-aware AI surrogate models
  • You have a cool research idea at the intersection with interesting literature and data to analyze
