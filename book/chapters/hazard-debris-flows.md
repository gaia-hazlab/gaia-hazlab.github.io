## Scientific framing

Debris flows are fast, destructive mixtures of water and sediment, often initiated on steep
or recently burned terrain when intense rainfall mobilizes loose material. **Fire-altered
soil properties** and short-duration rainfall intensity are dominant controls, and run-out
governs downstream impact.
