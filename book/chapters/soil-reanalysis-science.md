## Scientific framing

How water moves through soils — infiltration, redistribution, recharge, evapotranspiration,
and runoff — sets the state that all geohazards inherit. This page develops the
**water-flux and land-surface modeling** that turn observations into a physically consistent
soil reanalysis.
