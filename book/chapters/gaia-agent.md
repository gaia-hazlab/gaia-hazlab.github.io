# GaiaAgent

:::{note}
**The agentic-AI layer of GAIA.** GaiaAgent is the umbrella for the AI agents that work
alongside the [DataHub](datahub), [ModelHub](modelhub), and [HazEvalHub](hazevalhub) — bridging
disciplines, acquiring and staging data, and supporting reproducible research software.
:::

## Overview

GaiaAgent bundles GAIA's agentic-AI tools:

- **[GAIA Translator](gaia-translator)** — a cross-disciplinary geoscience question-answering
  assistant that bridges seismology, hydrology, geomorphology, and atmospheric and soil sciences.
- **[Agentic Data Downloader](gaia-agentic)** — agentic AI for discovering, downloading, and
  provenance-tracking the multi-source hazard data the digital twins consume.
- **[Research Software](research-software)** — the research-software-engineering agent and the
  open-source tooling ecosystem.

These agents share a common knowledge base and skill set.
