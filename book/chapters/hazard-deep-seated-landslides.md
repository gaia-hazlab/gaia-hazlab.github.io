## Scientific framing

Deep-seated landslides involve failure surfaces meters to tens of meters below the surface,
responding to **groundwater and hydraulic head** on seasonal-to-multiyear timescales rather
than individual storms. Their slow, memory-rich behavior ties them closely to water-table
estimation ([soil-memory](soil-memory), [groundwater & soil moisture](groundwater-soil-moisture)).
