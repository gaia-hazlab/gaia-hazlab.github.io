## Scientific framing

Floods arise when precipitation, snowmelt, and saturated ground exceed the landscape's
capacity to store and convey water. **Antecedent soil saturation** sets how much rainfall
becomes runoff, tightly linking flood susceptibility to the soil reanalysis and to
sediment transport from mountain to sea.
