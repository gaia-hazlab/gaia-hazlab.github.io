## Scientific framing

Rainfall-triggered shallow landslides occur when infiltration raises pore pressure and
lowers effective stress in the soil mantle above a failure plane. Their susceptibility is
strongly modulated by **antecedent soil moisture** and the **hydromechanical memory** of the
soil ([soil-memory](soil-memory)), making them a direct target of the soil reanalysis.
